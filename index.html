The Team

Meet Our Artists

Each artist brings a distinct voice and mastery of their craft. Collectively, we cover every style imaginable.

MR

Marcus Reeve

Black & Grey · Realism

With over 12 years of experience, Marcus specialises in hyper-realistic portraiture and dark illustrative work. His meticulous shading technique has earned him multiple convention awards.

SC

Sofia Cruz

Fine Line · Watercolour

Sofia's delicate linework and soft watercolour washes create pieces that feel like paintings on skin. She trained in Barcelona and brings a uniquely European sensitivity to her work.

KT

Kenji Tanaka

Japanese · Neo-Traditional

Kenji is a third-generation tattoo artist rooted in Irezumi tradition, reinterpreted with a modern eye. His large-format back pieces and full sleeves are nothing short of legendary.

AL

Amara Levi

Geometric · Dotwork

Amara turns mathematical precision into spiritual art. Her mandalas and sacred-geometry compositions are highly sought after, often booked months in advance.

Our Story

Craft, Passion &
Permanence

Dark Needle was born in 2015 from a simple conviction: that tattoo art deserves the same reverence as any fine art. Nestled in the heart of Los Angeles, our studio was built as a sanctuary — a space where clients feel safe, heard, and excited.

We don't chase trends. We chase mastery. Every artist on our team brings a unique perspective and an obsessive attention to detail, whether they're covering a square inch or an entire back.

  • ◈
    Sterile & Safe

    Single-use needles, autoclave-sterilised equipment, and full bloodborne-pathogen certification.

  • ◈
    Consultation First

    Every project starts with an in-depth consultation — in person or via video call.

  • ◈
    Lifetime Touch-Up

    We stand behind our work. Any fading within 12 months is touched up at no cost.
